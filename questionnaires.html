Debrief

You have now reached the end of the experiment. Well done for doing reaching this stage and thank you for participating! The purpose of this experiment was to investigate how exploratory behaviour in this task was associated with anxiety and depression symptoms.

During this task you were presented with different environments, with either a or low amount of energy points associated with each. The summer and winter appearances of the environments were matched to be associated with either high or low amount of energy points in the environment. Additionally, the rewards were concentrated on different objects in the environment for different trials: At times trees, at others, rivers and mountains. The reward would be concentrated on one of these element categories for 6 consecutive trials each.

Our aim was to measure how much people will explore the environment in different conditions, depending on their prior knowledge and experiences of the rewards associated with a different objects. Furthermore, we aim to understand if exploratory behaviour is correlated with mental health symptoms, as well as tendencies measured in the questionnaires.
